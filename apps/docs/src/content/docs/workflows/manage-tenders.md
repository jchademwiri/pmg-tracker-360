## Tender workflow at a glance

1. Capture or open a tender record.
2. Verify client, deadlines, and ownership.
3. Track progress through status updates.
4. Review outcomes and link downstream work.

## Recommended operating routine

- Start each day by reviewing upcoming submission dates.
- Update owner and status as work progresses.
- Add notes/context before handoffs.
- Keep fields complete to improve reporting quality.

## Data quality tips

- Keep tender names consistent.
- Avoid duplicate records.
- Ensure date fields are realistic and complete.
