---
title: Invitations and Roles
description: Add users and assign the correct access level in Tracker 360.
---

## Invite a teammate

1. Open the organization or team management area.
2. Send an invitation to the teammate's email.
3. Choose the minimum role required for their responsibilities.
4. Confirm they can access only the features they need.

## Role assignment guidance

- Assign least privilege first.
- Expand access only when a real workflow requires it.
- Review access quarterly or after team changes.

## If a user cannot access a feature

- Confirm the user accepted their invitation.
- Confirm they are in the correct organization/team context.
- Re-check role permissions with an admin.
