## Who this guide is for

Use this guide if you are new to Tracker 360 and need to start working quickly.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you have an active account.
- Confirm you can sign in to the Tracker app.
- Ask your admin which organization and role you should use.

## First login checklist

1. Sign in with your assigned account.
2. Open your dashboard and confirm you can see your organization data.
3. Visit the tenders area and open one tender detail page.
4. Visit projects and purchase orders to confirm access.

## Your first workflow

1. Open the tenders list.
2. Filter by status or due date.
3. Open a tender and review key dates and ownership.
4. Move to related project and PO records as needed.
