## Main areas

- **Dashboard:** high-level status and recent activity.
- **Tenders:** active opportunities and submission tracking.
- **Projects:** delivery lifecycle after tender outcomes.
- **Purchase Orders:** PO records, status, and related project context.
- **Settings:** profile and account-level preferences.

## How to move faster

- Use list filters first, then search.
- Open detail pages for status, owners, and timeline fields.
- Use consistent naming for tenders/projects so lists stay scannable.

## Access and permissions

If you do not see a menu item or cannot complete an action, your role may not include that permission. See role guidance in [Invitations and Roles](/team/invitations-and-roles/).
